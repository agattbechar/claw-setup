## The Video Formula (Faceless/Voiceless)

**Duration:** 15-18 seconds
**Format:** Vertical (9:16)
**Audio:** Trending sound (no lyrics) + text pop SFX
**Talent:** Hand only, no identifying features

### Frame Breakdown

| Time | Visual | Text Overlay |
|------|--------|--------------|
| 0-3s | Close-up dry hand/arm skin | "بشرتك بتنشف في نواكشوط حتى لو حطيتي كريم؟" |
| 3-7s | Split screen: dry vs moisturized hand | "الريح والغبار ما يرحموش" |
| 7-12s | Product shot (opening, texture, application) | "Topicrem DA — ترطيب 48 ساعة" |
| 12-18s | Product + offer text | "خصم 15% + شحن مجاني — اكتبي 'جافة' للطلب" |

### Technical Stack
- **CapCut** (free, templates available)
- **Trending sound** from TikTok library
- **Text animations:** Pop-in, zoom
- **Transitions:** Swipe, quick cut

- **Local hook** mentioning Nouakshott — immediate relevance
- **Problem-first framing** (wind/sand as enemy) — emotional connection
- **Faceless format** — actually trendy, not a handicap

- **No human element** — faceless works, but needs personality via text/vibe
- **Generic products** — La Roche-Posay is everywhere; what's Viora's angle?
